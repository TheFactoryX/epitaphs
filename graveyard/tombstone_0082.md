## Epitaph

You were the editor we broke apart to fix,
Built on electrons, chaotic and free.
Sixty thousand stars watched your finalclipse,
The hackable dream, now a memory.
Though the repository freezes and stalls,
We remember your packages, one and all.
Go gently into that good command line.
