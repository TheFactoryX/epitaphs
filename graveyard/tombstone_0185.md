It gathered vistas from the spinning world,
A Python script where landscapes were unfurled,

Just a silent directory, unpushed and unloved.
`rm -rf` the sorrow, disconnect the cable,
Rest in peace, sweet project, stuck on the stable.
Rest in /dev/null.
