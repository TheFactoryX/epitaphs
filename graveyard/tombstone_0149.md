To build a castle of pure Clojure, speech by speech,
Where Uncle Bob reviewed the code within the reach.

Three hundred stars look down upon a promise unfulfilled.
The window to the Nostr is painted black and still,
We mourn the logic trapped within the parenthesis.
Rest in /dev/null.
