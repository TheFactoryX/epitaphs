Once you mapped the flexbox lines,
A single page to save our minds.
But screens grow wide and interfaces change,
And nineteen hundred lines seem strange.
Silence has filled the open pull,
The starlight fades, the checks are null.
Commit your final syntax then,
Rest in /dev/null.
